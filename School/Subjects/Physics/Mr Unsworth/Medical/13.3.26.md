## Summary Questions
1) Name the main components of a CAT scanner:
	X-ray tube, detector, motorised sliding table
2) State one advantage and one disadvantage of a CAT scan over an X-ray image
	An advantage is that it provides depth information to the x-ray, however a disadvantage is that it greatly increases the exposure of the patient to x-rays, increasing risk of mutation.
3) Suggest why a thin beam of X-rays is necessary in a CAT scanner.
	To create a thin slice for an image

## Gamma camera
Produces images showing function and processes of body rather than anatomy
