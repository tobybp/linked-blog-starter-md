## Summary Qs
1) State a typical value for the wavelength of X-rays
	1x10$^{-10}$
2) Use the wavelength from question 1 to calculate:
	a) the frequency of the X-rays
		v = fλ
		3 x 10$^8$ / 1 x 10$^{-10}$ = 3 x 10$^{18}$
	b) the energy of a single X-ray photon
		e = hf
		e = 3 x 10$^8$ x 6.62 x 10$^{-34}$
		e = 1.986 x 10$^{-15}$
3) An x-ray tube is connected to a 65kV supply. Calculate:
	a) the kinetic energy of an electron
	e = Q x V
	= 1 x 10${-14}$J
